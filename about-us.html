About Us
The original Jarlsberg cheese is based on a secret Norwegian recipe from 1956, it famous based on the combination of traditional cheese-making and mordern technology gives the cheese an edge, appreciated by both world class and amateur chefs. There is a lot of stories behind the apperance of this kind of cheese, but for sure, it is an important one in the Norway cusine.
Back to us, we are both Vietnamese and suddently inspired by the taste of Jarlsberg cheese, it leads us to a common question that: Why not we bring it to Vietnam to create a chance for our beloved family, friends, Vietnamese can enjoy it with us? From our love for Vietnam and also Norway, we come up with a mission for Cheese of LN is that “Even faraway, everyone can taste from Norway”, and we decided to build up a business model to bring the best quality of Jarlsberg cheese in detail and Norway cusine in general into our beloved Vietnam. We have the key in delivery process to ensure the quality of the product and mainly exchange via online platform. There are a lot of stories behind this kind of cheese, and not only the food, but we also have an ambitious to deliver to customers our love, our message, our journey via each service, each piece of cheese.
