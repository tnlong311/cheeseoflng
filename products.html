About our products
Our main product is Jarlsberg original cheese comes from Norway. This kind of cheese is famous around the world for its distinctive sweet and nutty taste with the large shape and big round holes. Base on the diversity of customer needs, we also ensure the best quality of cheese in every shapes, even as 10kg wheel or cut to customer needs. Jarlsberg is a friendly kind of cheese, which means that even you but it as a regular for turito or stay healthy with salads, it still be a good choice for us to enjoy! Jarlsberg is naturally lactose-free and suitable for vegetarians.
Ingredients and storage
Jarlsberg is a friendly kind of cheese, it made from Paterurised cow’s milk, microbial enzyme (rennet), salt, bacterial culture, 27% fat (fat in dry matter 45%). And because of the sensitivity of the ingredients, Jarlsberg cheese is prefer to store at 0 °C - 4 °C and best sliced when cool.
